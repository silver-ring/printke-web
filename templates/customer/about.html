About PrintKe

Kenya's trusted partner for professional card printing since 2024

Our Story

PrintKe was founded with a simple mission: make professional card printing accessible to everyone in Kenya. We noticed that schools, companies, and organizations often struggled to get quality ID cards printed quickly and affordably. Many had to visit physical shops, wait in queues, and deal with inconsistent quality.

We changed that. With our online platform, you can order professional-quality CR80 PVC cards from anywhere in Kenya. Upload your design, pay via M-Pesa, and we'll print and deliver to your doorstep. It's that simple.

Our Mission

"To provide fast, affordable, and high-quality card printing services to every school, company, and organization in East Africa."

Why Choose Us

Professional Quality

We use Entrust card printers - the same technology used by banks for credit cards. Your cards will be durable, professional, and impressive.

Fast Turnaround

Orders placed before 2 PM are printed the same day. We understand urgency - especially for schools during admission season.

Nationwide Delivery

We deliver to all 47 counties in Kenya. No matter where you are, we'll get your cards to you.

Easy Payment

Pay instantly via M-Pesa. No bank transfers, no delays. Just confirm on your phone and we start printing.

Our Clients

We're proud to serve a diverse range of clients across Kenya:

  • Schools and universities (student ID cards)
  • Companies and corporations (employee badges)
  • Churches and religious organizations (membership cards)
  • SACCOs and cooperatives (member cards)
  • Gyms and fitness centers (membership cards)
  • Events and conferences (access badges)
  • Loyalty programs (customer cards)
