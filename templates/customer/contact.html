Frequently Asked Questions

What file format should I upload?

We accept PNG and JPG images. For best results, use 1012 x 638 pixels at 300 DPI. Our system will automatically resize your images to fit the CR80 card format.

How long does printing take?

Orders placed before 2 PM are printed the same day. Delivery depends on your location - same day in Nairobi CBD, next day for greater Nairobi, and 2-5 days for other regions.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! We have professional designers who can create your card design. Contact us via WhatsApp to discuss your requirements. Design services start from KES 2,000.

What's the minimum order quantity?

There's no minimum order - you can order even a single card. However, ordering more gives you better per-card pricing with our bulk discounts.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa payments via STK Push. Just enter your phone number and confirm the payment on your phone. For large orders, we can also accept bank transfers.

Can I get a sample before ordering bulk?

Yes! You can order a single card as a sample before placing a bulk order. This way you can verify the quality and colors before committing to a large order.
